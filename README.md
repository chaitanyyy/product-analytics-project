# 🛒 Product Analytics Dashboard
> End-to-end product analytics project analyzing 800K+ 
> e-commerce transactions to uncover customer behavior, 
> retention trends, and churn risk.

---

## 📌 Business Problem
An e-commerce company had no visibility into:
- Where customers drop off in the purchase journey
- Which customer cohorts retain best over time
- Which high-value customers are at risk of churning

---

## 💡 Solution
Built a complete product analytics pipeline from raw CSV 
data to an interactive Tableau dashboard covering:
✅ Customer purchase funnel analysis
✅ Monthly cohort retention heatmap
✅ Churn risk segmentation
✅ Revenue trend analysis

---

## 🔍 Key Findings
- 72% of customers made repeat purchases (healthy!)
- Dec 2009 cohort had best Month-1 retention (35%)
- 3,843 out of 5,878 customers are High Value (Revenue > £500)
- Revenue peaked in Nov 2011 at £1.2M
